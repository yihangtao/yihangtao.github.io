About Me

Yihang Tao received the B.S. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2021 and received his M.S. degree from the School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2024. Currently, he is pursuing his PhD degree in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (Advisor: Prof. Yuguang "Michael" Fang).

His current research interests include computer vision, generative models, and autonomous driving. He serves as the program committee member for CVPR, ICML, ICLR, ACM MM, and the reviewer for IEEE TITS, TMC, RA-L, etc.

I am actively seeking collaborators of like-minded interests. If you are interested in my work, please feel free to contact me via email: yihang.tommy@my.cityu.edu.hk.
