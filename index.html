About Me

Yihang Tao received the B.S. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2021 and received his M.S. degree from the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2024 (Advisor: Prof. Jun Wu). Currently, he is pursuing his PhD degree in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (Advisor: Prof. Yuguang “Michael” Fang). His current research interests include collaborative perception, decentralized multi-agent communication, driving digital twin and world model. He is the reviewer for IEEE LNET, Pattern Recognition, TPC member for IEEE GLOBECOM 2023-2024.
