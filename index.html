About Me

Yihang Tao received the B.S. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2021. He is currently pursuing the M.S. degree with the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. His research interests include digital twin networks, intelligent reflection surface, and RF energy harvesting. He is the reviewer for IEEE Networking Letters, TPC member and session chair for IEEE GLOBECOM 2023.
